Title: Jesus, stand among us, In thy risen power

[Verse 1] 
Jesus, stand among us, In thy risen power, Let this time of worship Be a hallowed hour.


[Verse 2] 
Breathe Thy Holy Spirit Into every heart, Bid the fears and sorrows From each soul depart. Thus with quickened footsteps We'll pursue our way, Watching for the dawning Of eternal day.
