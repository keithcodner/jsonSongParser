Title: Let us with a gladsome mind
Author: John Milton.
SongID: FfhY119ddkKyEfDTnp0Elg
Copyright: 

[Verse 1] 
Let us with a gladsome mind Praise the Lord, for He is kind: For His mercies shall endure, Ever faithful, ever sure.


[Verse 2] 
Let us blaze His name abroad, For of Gods He is the God: For His mercies shall endure, Ever faithful, ever sure.


[Verse 3] 
He, with all-commanding might, Filled the new-made world with light: For His mercies shall endure, Ever faithful, ever sure.


[Verse 4] 
He the golden-tressed sun Caused all day his course to run: For His mercies shall endure, Ever faithful, ever sure.


[Verse 5] 
And the silver moon by night, 'Mid her spangled sisters bright: For His mercies shall endure, Ever faithful, ever sure.


[Verse 6] 
He His chosen race did bless In the wasteful wilderness: For His mercies shall endure, Ever faithful, ever sure.


[Verse 7] 
All things living He doth feed, His full hand supplies their need: For His mercies shall endure, Ever faithful, ever sure.


[Verse 8] 
Let us with a gladsome mind Praise the Lord, for He is kind: For His mercies shall endure, Ever faithful, ever sure.
