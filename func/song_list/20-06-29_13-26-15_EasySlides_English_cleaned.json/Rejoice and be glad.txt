Title: Rejoice and be glad
Author: Horatius Bonar (1808-89)
SongID: 0AOz0OowdUCji+s8slb5vw
Copyright: Public Domain

[Verse 1] 
Rejoice and be glad! the Redeemer has come: go, look on His cradle, His cross, and His tomb.


[Verse 2] 
Sound His praises, tell the story of Him who was slain; sound His praises, tell with gladness He now lives again.


[Verse 3] 
Rejoice and be glad! it is sunshine at last; the clouds have departed, the shadows are past.
