[Verse 1] 
I clasp the hand of Love divine, I claim the gracious promise mine, and add to His my countersign, I take, He undertakes.


[Verse 2] 
I take Thee, blessed Lord, I give myself to Thee, and Thou, according to Thy word, dost undertake for me.


[Verse 3] 
I take salvation full and free, through Him who gave His life for me, He undertakes my All to be, I take, He undertakes.
