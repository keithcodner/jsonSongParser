[Verse 1] 
Oh, for a new anointing, oh, for a heavenly flame - oh, for a new anointing, to magnify Thy Name. Oh, for sin and self to cease, oh, for a sense of inward peace, oh, for thy glory to increase, Saviour, anoint me now.
