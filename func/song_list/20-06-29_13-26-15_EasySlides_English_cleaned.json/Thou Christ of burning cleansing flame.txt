Title: Thou Christ of burning cleansing flame
Author: William Booth
SongID: WAb3K08Ht0OiF4S5GLmVKA
Copyright: 

[Verse 1] 
Thou Christ of burning, cleansing flame, send the fire, send the fire! Thy blood-bought gift today we claim, send the fire, send the fire! Look down and see this waiting host, give us the promised Holy Ghost, we want another Pentecost, send the fire, send the fire!


[Verse 2] 
God of Elijah, hear our cry! Oh, make us fit to live or die! To burn up every trace of sin, to bring the light and glory in, the revolution now begin.


[Verse 3] 
'Tis fire we want, for fire we plead, the fire will meet our every need, for strength to ever do the right, for grace to conquer in the fight, for power to walk the world in white.


[Verse 4] 
To make our weak hearts strong and brave, to live a dying world to save; oh, see us on Thy altar lay our lives, our all, this very day; to crown the offering now, we pray
