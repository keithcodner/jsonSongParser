Title: Whosoever heareth!
Author: Philip Paul Bliss (1838-76)
SongID: plJnD/YBZ0aF9VV4U1myVQ
Copyright: 

[Verse 1] 
“Whosoever heareth!” shout, shout the sound! Send the blessed tidings all the world around! Spread the joyful news wherever man is found; “Whosoever will may come.”


[Verse 2] 
“Whosoever will!” “whosoever will!” Send the proclamation over vale and hill: ‘tis the loving Father calls the wanderer home: “Whosoever will may come.”


[Verse 3] 
Whosoever cometh need not delay; now the door is open, enter while you may: Jesus is the true and only Living Way, “Whosoever will may come.”


[Verse 4] 
“Whosoever will!” “whosoever will!” Send the proclamation over vale and hill: ‘tis the loving Father calls the wanderer home: “Whosoever will may come.”


[Verse 5] 
“Whosoever will,” the promise is secure; “Whosoever will,” for ever shall endure; “Whosoever will” - ‘tis life for evermore: “Whosoever will may come.”


[Verse 6] 
“Whosoever will!” “whosoever will!” Send the proclamation over vale and hill: ‘tis the loving Father calls the wanderer home: “Whosoever will may come.”
