Author: Josiah Conder

[Verse 1] 
Thou art the everlasting Word, the Father’s only Son; God manifestly seen and heard, and heaven’s Beloved One.


[Verse 2] 
Worthy, O Lamb of God, art Thou, that every knee to Thee should bow.


[Verse 3] 
In Thee most perfectly expressed the Father’s glories shine, of the full deity possessed, eternally divine! Worthy, O Lamb of God, art Thou, that every knee to Thee should bow.


[Verse 4] 
Worthy, O Lamb of God, art Thou, that every knee to Thee should bow.


[Verse 5] 
True image of the infinite, whose essence is concealed; brightness of uncreated light, the heart of God revealed. Worthy, O Lamb of God, art Thou, that every knee to Thee should bow.


[Verse 6] 
Worthy, O Lamb of God, art Thou, that every knee to Thee should bow.


[Verse 7] 
But the high mysteries of Thy name an angel’s grasp transcend; the Father only, glorious claim! the Son can comprehend. Worthy, O Lamb of God, art Thou, that every knee to Thee should bow.


[Verse 8] 
Worthy, O Lamb of God, art Thou, that every knee to Thee should bow.


[Verse 9] 
Yet loving Thee, on whom His love ineffable doth rest, Thy glorious worshippers above, as one with Thee are blest! Worthy, O Lamb of God, art Thou, that every knee to Thee should bow.


[Verse 10] 
Worthy, O Lamb of God, art Thou, that every knee to Thee should bow.
