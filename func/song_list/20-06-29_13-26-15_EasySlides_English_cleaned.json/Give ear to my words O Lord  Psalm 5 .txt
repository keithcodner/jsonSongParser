[Verse 1] 
Give ear to my words, O Lord. Consider my meditation. Hearken unto the voice of my cry, My King and my God. For unto Thee will I pray, My voice shalt Thou hear in the morning. Oh Lord, in the morning will I direct my prayer Unto Thee and will look up.
