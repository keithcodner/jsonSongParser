Title: More Love To Thee
Author: Elizabeth Prentiss William H. Doane
SongID: 0S4xBNrVIU2raB+7oAqxag
Copyright: Public Domain

[Verse 1] 
More love O Christ to Thee; More love to Thee, More love to Thee!


[Verse 2] 
More love to Thee, O Christ, More love to Thee! Hear Thou the prayer, I make on bended knee. This is my earnest plea:


[Verse 3] 
Once earthly joy I craved, Sought peace and rest. Now Thee alone I seek; Give what is best. This all my prayer shall be:


[Verse 4] 
Then shall my latest breath Whisper Thy praise. This be the parting cry My heart shall raise; This still my prayer shall be:


[Verse 5] 
Let sorrow do its work, Send grief and pain; Sweet are Thy messengers, Sweet their refrain, When they can sing with me:
